About Me

Olasile OBA by name. A citizen of Nigeria from Ogun state. I stay at Ibadan and I am a music producer with about 11 years experience.I make all kinds of genres [Afro beats,Trap,Hip Hop,UK Drill,Amapiano,R & B and many more...
I am also a mixing and mastering engineer that uses Cubase and FL studio as his favorite software.I am available for an type of music making,production or engineering. Hit me up.
I can also help with music business, like getting the good A/R for your project, work on project covers and so on. I am always ready to work anytime and anywhere in the world.Hit me up and let's discuss
